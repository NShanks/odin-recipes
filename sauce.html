Stuff you need

  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Blend onion, celery, and carrot together with olive oil
  2. Cook that in sauce pot for a while
  3. Add garlic
  4. Dump in crushed tomatoes
  5. Reduce a lot until not so watery
  6. Realize you forgot salt and pepper and add it now
  7. Also toss in that parsley
  8. Throw on pasta and go "mmmm, delicious"
